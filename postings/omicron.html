On January 5th the President and Provost announced an updated plan for the winter term in response to the rising cases of Omicron in Ontario.

As a result of this announcement, (Bio 3SS) will be returning to in-person lectures and tutorials during the week of February 7th. The times and rooms in your schedule have not changed.

Visit the Back-to-Mac resource guide for more information on what will be open and operating this January, in addition to steps being taken to support your health and safety.

There will be challenges we will face over the next few weeks as the pandemic continues and more people are required to isolate, manage their own health and care for family. We are committed to being as flexible as possible as our community navigates through this latest phase of COVID.  If you have or suspect you have COVID-19, please follow the guidance of public health. You can access the MSAF if you will need to take time away from your studies to get well, or if you must isolate as a result of a positive case in your home.

Before coming to campus, check your A2L course announcements to make sure there are no changes to the schedule. All community members are expected to complete daily health screening via MacCheck every time they access McMaster’s campus or an affiliated site.  Updates to MacCheck are in alignment with provincial requirements, including updates to symptoms.
